Chocolate
caliente.

Ingredients
  • 1 liter of milk
  • 1 chocolate tablet
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon (optional)
  • ¼ cucharadita de paprika (opcional)
    • ¼ teaspoon of paprika (optional)



    Preparation
    Heat a liter of milk in a pot over high heat; move constantly to avoid burning. ADD chocolate cake and mix until dissolved. POUR the preparation in the blender, cover and place a wet cloth on top (this will prevent the lid from being thrown out by the hot liquid) SMELL the chocolate for 5 minutes or until a good amount of foam has formed. SERVE careful not to undo the foam, add a little whipped cream on top; sprinkle cinnamon and paprika. If you do not have a chocolate tablet, you can use cocoa powder or semi-bitter chocolate and sweeten it to your liking.
