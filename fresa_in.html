Marmalade
Strawberry







  • Ingredients:

  • 1 kilo of strawberries, clean

  • 4 cups of white sugar

  • 1/4 cup of real lemon juice



    • Preparation

    1. In a large bowl, mash the strawberries little by little until you have 4 cups of crushed fruit. In a cast iron casserole, mix strawberries, sugar and lemon juice. Cook over low heat, continuing to move, until the sugar has dissolved. Raise the heat to high and let the mixture boil, continuing to move, until it reaches a temperature of 105 ° C (220 ° F).

    2. Pour into sterilized and hot jars, leaving about 1 cm of space between the jam and the neck of the jar. Close well. Cool in a water bath if you are going to consume immediately, otherwise, just refrigerate.
