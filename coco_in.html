Marmalade
Coconut







  • Ingredients:

  • 500 gr. of dry coconut without sweetening.

  • 2 tablespoons of orange blossom water or roses

  • 500 gr. of sugar

  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice

  • 100 gr. of blanched almonds or 100 gr. of crushed pistachios.

    • Preparation

    1. Peel the apples, remove the hearts and put them in small cubes. Put them in a casserole with sugar and lemon juice and let marinate for about 1 hour.


    2. Put the casserole over medium heat and let it cook for 35-40 minutes, stirring from time to time. When it has a texture of jam, remove the casserole from the heat. If you want a finer texture without lumps, you can crush the jam with the blender or the masher.


    3.Fill the glass jars previously sterilized little by little and close them well.


    4. To make the vacuum, put a cloth in the bottom of a casserole, place the jam jars on top and fill the casserole with water to cover them. Let boil about 30 minutes. Let the jars cool down, dry them well and do not forget to label them indicating the product and the date of preparation.


    5. Store the jam in a cool, dry place away from the light, where it will last several months in perfect condition.
