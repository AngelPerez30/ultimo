Marmalade
Blueberries







  • Ingredients:

  • 300 g of fresh blueberries

  • 150 g of sugar

  • 100 ml of water

    • Preparation

    you should do is clean the fruits well. Put them all in a bowl and rub them lightly with water, helping your hands to be clean, but being careful not to ruin them.


    When they are clean, put the cranberries, sugar and water in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir so that the sugar dissolves and let the ingredients cook.


    As the mixture begins to boil, foam will start to come out. Help yourself with a spoon to remove it.


    Allow the content to reduce for about 30 minutes until it thickens. You must bear in mind that once the blueberry jam is cold it will thicken more.


    A trick to know if the jam is really ready is to take a little with a spoon, throw it on a plate and divide it in two. If the jam comes back together it is still not ready and, if on the contrary, it stays divided, it will be at its point.


    Once the blueberry jam is ready, you only have to put it in sterilized jars, make sure the jar is completely dry and store it in a cool and dry place.
