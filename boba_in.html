Boba







  • Ingredients:

  • 4 ¼ cups of milk

  • ¼ cup of granulated sugar

  • 1 cinnamon stick

  • 1 ¼ cup of tapioca

  • 1 ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon

    • Preparation


    1. Combine milk with sugar and cinnamon stick.


    2. Boil the milk and add the tapioca.


    3. Cook over medium heat for 20 minutes or until the tapioca is transparent.

    6. Serve in dessert glasses and sprinkle powdered cinnamon on top.
