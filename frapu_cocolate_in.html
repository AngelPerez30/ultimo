Drink
Chocolate frappuccino





  • Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cups of cold strong coffee

  • 1/4 cup of chocolate ice cream

  • 1/4 cup of chocolate sauce

  • 2 cups (separated) of crushed ice whipped cream to decorate

    • Preparation


    In a blender or mixer combine coffee with chocolate ice cream and chocolate sauce. Process 1 or 2 minutes.


    In two tall glasses divide one of the cups of crushed ice. Add the other cup of crushed ice to the blender.


    Process until a thick mixture forms (sometimes I like to add more ice, or use the ice that you put in the glasses). Serve in tall glasses and garnish with whipped cream.
