Pay
Coconut







  • Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of milk

  • 1 cup of sugar

  • 4 eggs

  • 1/2 cup flour

  • 6 tablespoons of butter

  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract

  • 1/2 tablespoon of salt

  • 1 cup of grated sweet coconut

    • Preparation

    In a blender mix the first 7 ingredients for 10 seconds.

    Spoon the part of the mixture that sticks to the blender wall and liquefy another 10 seconds.

    Add the Coco and blend for another 2 seconds or until well blended.

    Empty it in a mold for Pay.

    Bake at 350 degrees for 50 to 55 minutes or until you insert a toothpick into the Pay and come out clean.
