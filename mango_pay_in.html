Pay
Mango







  • Ingredients:

  • 1 can of condensed milk

  • 1 package of cream cheese (190 gr)

  • 1 ½ cups chopped mango (4 mangoes)

  • 1 on unsweetened gelatin or gelatin (7 gr)

  • ¼ cup water

  • 1 base or crust of pay (of cookies)

    • Preparation

    1.Peel and cut the mangoes into cubes. You need 1 ½ cups of mango which is an approximate of 4 small mangoes, but it is best to cut into cubes and measure the mango so you can make sure it is the amount that the recipe uses.

    2.Mix the grenetine with the water and let stand 5 minutes. After the time you should see the mixture thick. Heat for 20 to 30 seconds in the microwave or melt in a water bath.

    3.Place the condensed milk, cream cheese, chopped mango and hydrated greenery in the blender jar. Blend until very smooth and smooth.

    4.Empty in the pie pan and refrigerate for 4 to 5 hours or until the pie has thickened and set completely. It should look firm so you can cut it easily.

    5.Serve this way alone or decorate with a little more chopped mango.
