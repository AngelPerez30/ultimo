Pay
Kiwi







  • Ingredients:

  • 350 grams of butter

  • 1 pinch of salt

  • 375 grams of sugar

  • vanilla to taste

  • 500 grams of flour

  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

  • 125 grams of strawberry cut in half

  • 1/2 cup finely chopped walnut

  • 3 pieces of egg

  • 1 cup of cream

  • 250 grams of peeled kiwi, cut into slices and then in half

    • Preparation

    For the base, mix all the ingredients and knead until you obtain a smooth and homogeneous dough. Refrigerate a little (15 minutes approximately) and then, with the help of a rolling pin, cover a pie pan with this dough.


    For the filling, mix all the ingredients except for the fruit and beat well until you get a smooth and homogeneous cream. Serve this shake inside the base of the raw pie and bake in the oven previously heated for 45 minutes at 150ËšC. Let cool and decorate with strawberries and kiwi.
