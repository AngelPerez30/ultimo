Cake
walnut and chocolate

Ingredients
  • 1 cup peeled walnut
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 150 grams of semi-bitter chocolate
  • 1 spoon of vanilla
  • 1 tablespoon of milk
  • 1/2 liter of whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar glass
  • 1 butter square



    • Preparation
    Preheat the oven to 180 c. In the blender, liquefy nuts, eggs, condensed milk and flour. Put the mixture in a previously greased cake refractory. Bake 30-35 minutes. Melt chocolate in a bain-marie with milk and butter. In another bowl, beat the cream with the icing sugar and vanilla until it hardens. When the cake is ready, come out of the oven, let it cool to room temperature. Top with the melted chocolate and then with the whipped cream. Almond cake
