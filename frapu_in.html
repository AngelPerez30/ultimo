Drink
Frappuccino





  • Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of instant coffee (cappuccino type)

  • 120 ml of milk

  • 2 tablespoons of sugar

  • 6 ice cubes

  • Chantilly cream to decorate on top

    • Preparation


    Put all the ingredients in the blender and process until it is like a frozen cream.


    Serve in a tall glass and top with chantilly cream, grated chocolate, syrup of some flavor to taste, strawberries, cinnamon or whatever you like..
