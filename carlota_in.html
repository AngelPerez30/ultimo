Carlota
Lemon







  • Ingredients:

  • 6 pieces of lemon

  • 1 can of evaporated milk (360 ml)

  • 1 can of condensed milk

  • 2 packages of Mary biscuit (170 g)

    • Preparation

    Squeeze the lemons and reserve the juice.


    Blend evaporated milk, condensed milk and lemon juice until you get a cream.


    In a medium bowl, place the cookies and add the previous mixture, try to form layers of cookies and the mixture you prepared. Fill the mold perfectly.


    Decorate with pieces of biscuit or sliced lemon.


    Refrigerate for 2 hours and serve.
