Drink
Original frappuccino





  • Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of freshly made espresso coffee

  • 2 and a half cups of low-fat milk (2%)

  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

  • 1 spoonful of pectin * * This ingredient serves to make the drink thicker and creamier, it is a natural ingredient coming from fruits and it can be ignored if it is not so interesting that it has the consistency of the original frappuccino.

    • Preparation


    Put all ingredients in a jar or covered container, shake or mix well until everything is incorporated.


    Serve very cold.


    Always decorate with whipped cream and cinnamon, or grated chocolate, etc.


    From my point of view to this recipe I would add some ice and process it in the blender. In this way an even colder drink remains, almost as if it were an ice cream. But personally, what I like most is the texture of frappuccino!
