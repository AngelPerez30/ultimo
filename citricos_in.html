Marmalade
Citrus







  • Ingredients:

  • Water 3.5 L

  • Large oranges 2 Units

  • 1/4 cup of real lemon juice

  • Sugar 2.75 kg

  • Files 2 Units

    • Preparation

    Clean the citrus with boiling water, brush and dry with a cloth. Squeeze the fruit to obtain the juice. Reserve the seeds and skins. Cut the peel in julienne. On a canvas, place the skins and seeds of the three citrus fruits. Form a bag and tie tightly with a cotton thread. Arrange this bag in a copper pot, add the julienne cut peel and the citrus juice together with the water. Bring to a boil until it boils. Cook over low heat stirring occasionally, for 30 to 40 minutes or until the husks are tender. The mixture should be reduced to 1/3 of its initial volume. Remove the bag and squeeze the juice into the pot. Add the sugar and return to the fire. Stir continuously until completely melted and continue cooking without stirring for 20 to 25 minutes or until it reaches its gel point. Remove from heat and let stand until a thin film forms on the surface. Pack in previously sterilized bottles, seal with paraffin wax, let cool and finally cover.
